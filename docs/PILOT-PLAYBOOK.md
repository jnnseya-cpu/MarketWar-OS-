# MarketWar OS — 30-Day Growth Sprint (Pilot Playbook)

**For the pilot company. One brand, 30 days, real customers, real money — proven in the dashboard.**

The rule: **win with owned channels first** — your existing audience, email, WhatsApp,
referrals, a landing page. That is where real money comes *fast and free*. Paid ads come
later. You do not need any external ad account to get your first customers here.

The goal of the sprint is not "use the software." It is: **produce real enquiries and real
sales, and see every one attributed in your dashboard** — so at day 30 you can point to a
number and say *"MarketWar OS got us X customers and £Y."*

---

## Week 1 — Diagnose, strategise, build the offer (Days 1–5)

| Day | Module | Do this | You get |
|-----|--------|---------|---------|
| 1 | **Onboarding → Failure Audit** | Enter your business, run the audit | **Baseline "before"** — screenshot the scores. This is your proof anchor. |
| 1–2 | **Strategy Agents** (run all 7 in order) | Avatar → Message → Channels → 90-Day Content → Funnel → Paid-Ads → 30-Day Battle Plan | A clear target customer, message and 3-channel plan |
| 2 | **Offer Builder** | Build ONE irresistible, **time-limited** offer | The single biggest lever for fast customers |

> The offer is everything. *"20% off — this week only"* or *"first order free delivery"*
> beats any clever ad. Make it easy to say yes and hard to ignore.

## Week 1–2 — Build the customer-getting assets (Days 4–10)

| Module | Build |
|--------|-------|
| **Conversion Architect / Landing Pages** | A real hosted landing/order page for the offer, with a lead-capture form |
| **Brand Studio + Content Factory** | 5–10 posts + images + one short video promoting the offer |
| **Email Center** | A welcome + 3-step nurture sequence |
| **WhatsApp Center** | Fast-reply templates for enquiries |

Everything the AI generates is **real and usable** — you post/send it (publishing is manual
until the social connector is switched on).

## Week 2–4 — Execute & convert (Days 8–30) — *this is where money happens*

**Drive traffic to the landing page using FREE / owned channels:**

1. **Broadcast the offer to your existing contacts** — email list + WhatsApp.
   *(Fastest money: past customers + a strong offer.)*
2. **Post the generated content** organically — your social pages, Google Business, local groups.
3. **Referral push** — ask happy customers to refer for a reward.
4. **Local SEO page** — "[your service] in [your city]".

**Then convert every enquiry:**

- Each lead → log in **Customer Vault** → follow up **within the hour** (WhatsApp/email sequence).
- **Lead Recovery** → chase the ones who didn't buy yet.
- When someone buys → **Revenue → "Log a result"** (or send them a **tagged checkout link** so
  the payment self-attributes).

## What is manual right now (honest expectations)

- **Posting/publishing is manual** — the OS writes it, you post it. Auto-publish to 15 channels
  activates when the social connector key is set.
- **Payments** — log results manually, or connect payments so checkout links auto-attribute revenue.
- **Paid-ad connectors** (Meta/Google) are not wired yet — and you don't need them for the 30-day
  win, which is owned-channel-driven (faster and free).

## Daily rhythm (pin this)

- **Every morning:** check **Daily Briefing** → do the top action.
- **Every enquiry:** log it in Customer Vault, reply within the hour.
- **Every sale:** log it in Revenue so the dashboard stays true.
- **Every Friday:** open Command Center → screenshot the week's numbers.

30 screenshots later, you have a case study.
