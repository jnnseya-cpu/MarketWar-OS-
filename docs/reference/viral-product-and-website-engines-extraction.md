Developer Architecture

Implement both features as independent AI agents within the OS:

1. AI Viral Product Engine – vision-based analysis that transforms uploaded product images into conversion-focused marketing assets and campaigns.


2. AI Website Marketing Intelligence Engine – website crawling and analysis that converts an entire business website into a unified, AI-generated marketing strategy with branded creative assets, funnels, content, competitive intelligence, and publish-ready campaigns.



Together, these features enable a user to go from a single product photo or a website URL to a complete, multi-channel marketing campaign in minutes, dramatically reducing the time and expertise required to launch effective marketing.
